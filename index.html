About PyJaipur

PyJaipur is a non-profit Official Local Python User Group/Community for Jaipur Developers. Our community consists of Students, IT professionals, Software Developers, and Entrepreneurs. We aim to promote Python and FOSS (Free and Open Source Software) in Jaipur and nearby region. Whether you are a Student, Teacher, Developer, Entrepreneur or anything we have always something for you in our meetups.
